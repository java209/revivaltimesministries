REVIVAL TIME MINISTRIES

Bishop & First Lady Cooper


Bishop Dr. G. G. Cooper, a Jamaican by birth migrated to Canada and became a Canadian citizen. He received a Divine supernatural call from God to preach and evangelize at an early age. Bishop Cooper was Divinely inspired by God to return to his homeland, Jamaica during the social and political troublesome times of the 1980s. Human and capital flight, social disorder and corruption was the order of the day but traveling on a Divine Revelation Bishop Cooper returned to establish a Ministry which now has seven assemblies across the island, cable, radio, prison ministry and various other outreach ministries.

Apostle & Lady Saunders


The establishment of this church was the result of Edward and Evelyn Saunders coming from Vernon B.C. in May of 1951 and holding a series of revival meetings with a small group of Sabbath-keepers who were meeting together in the home of Brother and sister Issac Dyck on the Brown Road in Langley B.C. God graciously poured out His Spirit in these meetings. There was a wonderful, Spiritual awakening and an immediate interest created in the area for the establishment of a full-Gospel Sabbath-keeping Church uninhibited by denominational legislation. Consequently, meetings were continued in the Vasa Hall in Aldergrove B.C. where once again God sent a visitation of His Power and an outpouring of His Spirit.
